Usecase Name: Add Product To Website

Actors: Admin

Entry Condition: Admin must be logged into his account
Exit Condition: Product is added to Database successfully

Flow of events: 
1- Admin chooses add new product choice
               2- system prompts him for product details
3- Admin enters the product details
               4- System adds the product to the database
Exceptions: 
3- Admins enter invalid product details
              4- sytem shows a message for him to re-enter the details
